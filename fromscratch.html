United States National Parks

I am a major geek when it comes to the national parks. I love visiting them, reading about their histories and unique features, looking at images of them, and planning (usually hypothetical) trips to go visit them.

Unsurprisingly, then, the number of parks I have actually visited is far less than the number I hope to have visited by the end of my life. Below, I will list all the national parks I have already visited (some multiple times), followed by the parks I most want to visit but haven't yet.
